This website was created by Ketaav Chopra, a 16-year-old who developed this project as a personal initiative. Inspired by his mother's struggle with arthritis and the lack of affordable resources combining Ayurveda and yoga for pain relief, Ketaav sought to provide accessible solutions. Many websites offering similar services are costly, often charging 10-20 euros, and primarily focus on selling products. In contrast, this platform offers affordable courses, with prices capped at 5.00 euros. All proceeds are donated to charity, making this a nonprofit venture dedicated to helping others.
